Investing Basics

Successful investing involves buying low and selling high. Look for stocks with promising growth potential based on market trends. Diversify your portfolio across different business sectors and investment types (stocks/bonds). At Prudential, we prioritize sustainable investments that deliver Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) benefits. Learn more about ESG Integration and its categories in the dedicated ESG Integration tab.

ESG Integration

ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) factors are crucial in investment decisions. Environmental considerations focus on climate change mitigation and environmental impact. Social factors ensure financial security and employee welfare. Governance emphasizes corporate responsibility and ethical behavior. Prudential integrates ESG factors into our investment strategies to enhance long-term asset returns and uphold our core values.

Sustainable Investments

Explore various types of sustainable investments:

  • ESG-labeled debt: Green bonds, Social bonds, Sustainability bonds, Sustainability-linked bonds
  • Social Investments: Infrastructure, essential services, affordable housing, financial inclusion, sustainable food systems, employment generation

Investment Restrictions

To uphold proper ESG integration and investment policies, avoid investing in:

  • Thermal Coal: Companies generating more than 25% of revenue from thermal coal
  • Controversial Weapons: Manufacturers of cluster munitions, anti-personnel mines, biological, or chemical weapons
